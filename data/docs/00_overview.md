# Top-Tier University Admissions (Demo Knowledge Base)

This demo knowledge base describes a *generic* top-tier university admissions process.
It is not affiliated with any institution.

**Typical components**
- Application form + basic profile
- Academic record (transcripts, coursework)
- Standardized testing (if applicable to policy)
- Essays / personal statements
- Recommendations
- Activities & honors
- Optional interview (varies)

**Important note**
- Admissions decisions are made by the university and involve human review.
- This system does not provide legal advice, guarantees, or admission predictions.
